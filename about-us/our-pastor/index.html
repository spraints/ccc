Pastor Tim Grasham

Family: My wonderful wife, Susan, and I have been married for almost 23 years. We have 3 sons: Isaac, Riley, and Eli. We also have 2 very happy and loving dogs, Molly & Bailey.


Education: I graduated from Johnson University (formerly Johnson Bible College) in 1996 with a B.A. in Bible/Preaching.


How long have you been at Community: We moved to Frankfort in July of 2009 when I accepted the call to serve as pastor here at Community.


Favorite Books: The Present Future by Reggie McNeal, An Unstoppable Force by Erwin McManus, Sticky Teams by Larry Osborne, Emotionally Healthy Spirituality by Peter Scazzero, anything written by Francis Chan. I also enjoy reading historical novels.


Hobbies: I love sports. I enjoy Colts football, Cubs baseball, and just about anything related to the Purdue Boilermakers.....BOILER UP!! I also enjoy hanging out in the local coffee shops reading or chatting with friends.


What are you passionate about: After living in Frankfort for almost 10 years, it hit me like a ton of bricks that I LOVE FRANKFORT!! I am passionate about being an active part of our community and showing the love of Jesus in a very authentic way. No pretense! No "churchiness"! Just Jesus!


Tim is currently on sabbatical. If you have questions, feel free to call the church (654-9260), email elders@frankfortccc.com, or find us on Facebook.
